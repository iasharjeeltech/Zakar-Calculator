Zakat Kya Hai?
Zakat Islam ke 5 Arkan mein se teesra Rukn hai. Har us Muslim par farz hai jiske paas nisab se zyada maal ho aur 1 lunar year (Hawl) guzar gaya ho.

Quran (At-Tawbah 9:103):
"Khuz min amwalihim sadaqatan tutahhiruhum..." "Un ke maal mein se sadaqah lo jo unhe pak kare aur barokah de."

Zaroori Baatein
✅ Hawl: Poora 1 lunar year maal paas raha ho.
✅ Niyyat: Dene se pehle niyyat zaroori hai.
✅ 22K Jewellery: 22K rate se calculate karein.
✅ Zakat Ramadan mein: Afzal hai, par kisi bhi din de sakte hain.
❌ Parents/Children/Spouse: Inhe Zakat nahi de sakte.
❌ Rehne wala ghar: Ghar ki Zakat nahi hoti.
❌ Personal use items: Car, furniture etc. exempt hain.
